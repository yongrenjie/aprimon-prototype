Aprimon should be listed one per line as <BALL> <SPECIES>, e.g.

Beast Magikarp
Dream Togepi

Capitalisation as well as punctuation is ignored, but the parser is sensitive to typos in names so please ensure that everything is spelt correctly.

Regional forms can be entered in several different ways. All of the following should work (plus any form of punctuation or none):

Diglett A
Diglett Alola
A Diglett
Alolan Diglett
